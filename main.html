Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light." It is famous for its rich history, iconic landmarks, romantic atmosphere, and world-class art, fashion, and cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic iron tower offering panoramic views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless art treasures.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Stunning Gothic architecture with historical significance.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – White-domed church located at the top of Montmartre hill.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue with shops, cafes, and theaters.
  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – Classic French dish of cooked snails with garlic butter.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweet treats in many flavors.
  • Ratatouille – Traditional vegetable stew from Provence.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
